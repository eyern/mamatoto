A little about Us

Welcome to Mama Toto Events

Mama Toto Events specializes in creating uplifting experiences tailored to women's mental health and wellness. Our events seamlessly blend therapeutic practices with engaging and fun activities, providing a safe space for women to prioritize self-care and connect with others on a deeper level. From book clubs and dinner sessions to creative workshops and outdoor adventures, we curate unique experiences that promote healing, growth, and joy.

"Over 15 Years of Accomplishments”

Redefining self-care for women with a unique blend of fun and mental health wellness.

At Mama Toto Events, we believe that laughter, connection, and self-discovery are essential ingredients for a fulfilling life. Join us on a journey of empowerment and joy as we create unforgettable experiences designed to nourish your mind, body, and soul. Get ready to embark on a journey of laughter, growth, and sisterhood.
