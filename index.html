    Our Team

    Our Vision At Mama Toto Events, we believe that laughter, connection, and self-discovery are essential ingredients for a fulfilling life. Join us on a journey of empowerment and joy as we create unforgettable experiences designed to nourish your mind, body, and soul.

    Our Objectives

    Advocacy

    At Mama Toto Events we conduct advocacy programs for women's mental health and wellness through fun activities

    Enlightenment

    To conduct enlightenment programs for the Mama Toto Events Community, as we create unforgettable experiences.

    Empowerment

    The aim of Mama Toto Events is to empower women to make better decisions and build their self-confidence through dialogue that encourages them to identify and solve their own problems.

    Engaging Activities

    providing a safe space for women to prioritize self-care and connect with others on a deeper level.

    Purpose

    creating uplifting experiences tailored to women's mental health and wellness.

    Networking

    We believe that laughter, connection, and self-discovery are essential ingredients for a fulfilling life for every mother.

    Our Mission

    Mama Toto Events specializes in creating uplifting experiences tailored to women's mental health and wellness. Our events seamlessly blend therapeutic practices with engaging and fun activities, providing a safe space for women to prioritize self-care and connect with others on a deeper level.
